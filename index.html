About Spargat Group

Spargat Group is an innovative team dedicated to developing alternative solutions for the Blockman GO ecosystem, with focus on cross-platform accessibility, customization, and open-source technology for the community. We're known for creating Blockman Launcher and CubicalCore, among other projects.
