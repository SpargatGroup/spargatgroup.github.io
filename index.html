Who We Are

Spargat Group is a passionate, community-driven organization committed to enhancing the Blockman GO experience. We develop open-source tools and platforms that empower players and creators, ensuring the ecosystem remains accessible, dynamic, and innovative.

Our Philosophy

We believe in the power of open collaboration, prioritizing transparency, community feedback, and user-centric design in all our endeavors.

Community Focus

Our initiatives are born from community needs and driven by a desire to give back, preserve beloved experiences, and build for the future, together.

Driving Innovation

Constantly exploring new technologies and approaches to provide robust, performant, and adaptable solutions for the Blockman GO platform.
