Project Approach & Code Access

Our core projects are developed under a closed-source model to maintain focus and integrity. Source code access is provided on a need-to-know basis to trusted volunteers and dedicated developers for specific modules or components relevant to their contributions.

This ensures that while collaboration is key, the central vision and quality of our projects are upheld. We value transparency in our goals and community engagement, even with this development model.

Contributor Privileges

As an active contributor, you may be granted privileges including:

  • Access to specific project repositories for creating public/private servers (e.g., for CubicalCore based projects).
  • Permission to utilize designated code snippets or libraries for related developer projects, with appropriate attribution.
  • Potential access to development databases or testing environments relevant to team projects.

Code of Conduct & Contribution

To ensure a productive and secure development environment, we have a few key guidelines:

  • Confidentiality: No unauthorized sharing or leaking of project source code or internal development details.
  • Active Engagement: Consistent and meaningful contributions are expected. Developers should aim for regular commits, especially within a monthly cycle for active projects.
  • Technical Foundation: A good understanding of relevant languages and tools is essential (see Skills Required).
  • Attribution: When utilizing Spargat Group code or assets in your own projects (where permitted), proper copyright notice and credit to Spargat Group are mandatory.

Essential Skills & Experience

We are looking for individuals with:

  • Proficiency in one or more of the following: C, Java, Kotlin, C++, Bash.
  • Strong experience with Git version control.
  • Experience or strong interest in game development, server management, or related backend systems.
  • Ability to collaborate effectively within a remote team environment.
  • A proactive approach to problem-solving and continuous learning.
  • Passion for the Blockman GO community and open-source principles (even if our core is closed-source).
